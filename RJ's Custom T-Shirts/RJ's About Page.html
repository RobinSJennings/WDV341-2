Welcome to RJ's Custom T-shirts
A place where you can order any size t-shirt, with any color, and have your custom print or text printed on your t-shirt

Welcome to the #1 outlet for custom t-shirts, RJ's Custom T-Shirts. We have been in the custom t-shirt business since 1330 A.D. We started out making t-shirts for peasants, wenches, and even some royalty. Our most popular custom t-shirt then, was a potato sack with "Merlin is my homie" embroidered on the front. The Medici Family/Organization, were big customers too. In that time period, we were selling in record numbers to the Medici's. In the 1970's, we were the one of the top competetors with selling tie-dye peace sign shirts. Today, we print for every demographic, and for all ages. We stock all sizes and colors on a every-other-day basis to ensure that we never run out. But of course not one shred of any of these satistics or facts are remotely true. Let us know what you want and we can print it for you!
